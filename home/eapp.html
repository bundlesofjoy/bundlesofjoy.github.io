Definition of Words
DEFINITION
CLADOGRAM (n.)
A representation of a hypothetical relationship between groups of organisms. The term “cladogram” comes from the Greek words clados, meaning “branch” and gramma, which means “character”.

EXPLICATION
CURRENT (n.)
Current is defined as the rate at which charge flows through a surface (the cross section of a wire, for example). Despite referring to many different things, the word current is often used by itself instead of the longer, more formal "electric current". The adjective "electric" is implied by the context of the situation being described. The phrase "current through a toaster" surely refers to the flow of electrons through the heating element and not the flow of slices of bread through the slots.
CLARIFICATION
ANIMATION (n.)
Many people are confused about the concept of animation. It is a form of art in which objects such as drawings, images, or figures are manipulated to create motions that can convey information, stories, or entertainment. Animation artists use various 2D or 3D elements to create a distinctive display of moving graphics using a digital device. It simulates the movement and actions created by a series of drawn pictures, models, or puppets that creates the illusion of moving when combined together.
