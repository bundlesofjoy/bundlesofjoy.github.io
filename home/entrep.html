1. Executive Summary
Bundles of Joy is a start-up business run by a group of six Grade 12 STEM students of Pasig City Science High School (PCSHS). The company envisions to become the most convenient food company for every Filipino that satisfies their food cravings through high quality, tasty, and budget-friendly products. With this, its central mission is to cater the trendy food cravings of their customers with its wide variety of sweet and savory food choices in a food bundle.

Bundles of Joy offers customizable food bundle sets that can also be sold separately according to the customers' preferences. Most of the food variety that the company includes in their menu are the ones that most of the people crave or want to try whenever they encounter it through their social media accounts such as TikTok and Facebook. The company caters sweet foods, savory foods, and drinks that are unique, fresh, and new to the public. Bundles of Joy's main target market are teenagers and young adults; however, the company is also perfect for the general public as well as the best food brings out everyone's best mood.

Based on the data gathered, the five existing popular competitors are not able to meet the demand of the market due to demand increase as well as lack of food varieties since 4 out of the 5 competitors focus on sweet products. This means that the demand of customers and the demand with the food varieties were not met and due to this concern, Bundles of Joy were created. Facebook is used as the main platform in promoting the products and bundles. Other digital applications such as Messenger and Google Forms are utilized for inquiries concerning availing the products especially for customization of bundles. This includes the instructions and information regarding the order and delivery process. Only limited slots are available since the group wants to make sure the food’s quality remains fresh. Moreover, to improve the market strategy and to cope up to the consumers demand, Bundles of joy are open to gather suggestions and recommendations to accommodate the majority of our target market. The main goal of this business is to provide quality and convenient experience to the consumers by presenting affordability and good quality products. Bundles of Joy’s greatest advantage from its competitors is it offers a large variety of food choices at a budget-friendly price and its eco-friendly packaging.

The starting capital of the company is ₱10,701.85 . Assuming that there will be 216 orders within a week and there will be an 15% weekly increase in orders, the ending cash balance on the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th week will be ₱12,122.71, ₱13,941.12, ₱16,032.29, and ₱18,437.13, respectively. In conclusion, the company could make a net profit of ₱7,094.88 in its first week from their products with a starting capital of ₱10,701.85.

3. Product/Service Plan
Purpose of the Product

Bundles of Joy aims to provide affordable yet high quality trendy foods to satisfy the general public's cravings without burdening their pockets and give them the freedom of customising their own food bundles according to their likings.

Product’s Unique Features

Bundles of Joy's products exhibit three unique features. The first one is that it caters to the variety of trendy food cravings of the general public by putting them in a food bundle so the customers could try them out in one go. The second unique feature is the freedom of the customers to customize the content of their food bundles in their own likings. And lastly, the best unique feature the business has is its menu selection that contains with-a-twist food selections that are fresh and new to everyone's eyes.

Production Process and Controls that will be used to manufacture the product

Choco Mallows Lumpia
  1. Spread the chocolate on the lumpia wrapper.
  2. Cut the marshmallow on smaller pieces and put it above the chocolate.
  3. Wrap and close the combination of the two into the lumpia wrapper.
  4. Prepare the oil and frying pan.
  5. Fry the Choco mallows lumpia in the oil and make sure to watch the two.
Corn Dog
  1. Mix 200 g all-purpose flour with 10 g baking powder, 33 g milk powder, 2 tbsp white sugar, 1 medium-sized egg, and 100 g tap water in a mixing bowl. Then, transfer it in a tall cup of glass.
  2. Slice the hotdogs into two. Then, slice the cheese the size of half of the hotdog. Place the hotdog and cheese slice in the barbecue stick respectively.
  3. Dip the hotdog and cheese in the batter placed in the tall cup of glass. Then, roll it in breadcrumbs.
  4. Preheat the oil in a frying pan and then start frying the corndog. Fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve.
Fried Noodles
  1. Make the sauce by mixing the noodle sauce powder in hot or warm water until the mixture reaches its consistency.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a frying pan to high heat.
  3. Add the bean sprouts and stir fry for a minute or more.
  4. Add the egg noodle and stir for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add approximately 2 tbsp dark soy sauce. Stir-fry until noodles become a consistent dark brown color.
  6. Add spring onion or sesame seeds (optional).
Fried Oreos
  1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 375F (190C).
  2. Make the pancake batter: In one bowl mix flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. In the second bowl mix milk, butter, and egg.
  4. Combine dry mixture with wet mixture, and stir until smooth.
  5. Dip Oreos in the batter with a fork and then place in hot oil. Fry until golden, about 30-60 seconds, flip and fry for 30-60 seconds more.
  6. Transfer to a paper towel to drain excess oil, and (optional) dust with powdered sugar before serving.
Mango Graham Ice Cream Sandwich
  1. Put 500 mL of all purpose cream in the freezer overnight.
  2. Whip the all purpose cream for 3 minutes until the volume doubles using the electric hand mixer (set the mixer to the lowest level).
  3. Add 170 g of condensed milk in the all purpose cream and whip the mixture for another 3 minutes.
  4. Cut 419 g of mangoes into cubes and mix it in the mixture.
  5. Add the 64 g of crushed graham crackers as well in the mixture and mix well.
  6. In a tray, put the first layer of graham crackers upside down.
  7. Spread the mixture evenly and top it off with the second layer of the graham crackers the usual way (not upside down).
  8. Put the output in the freezer for 24 hours.
Milo Pastillas
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the condensed milk and melted butter. Mix until well-incorporated.
  2. In a separate mixing bowl, pour in the powdered milk and the Milo powder.
  3. Combine the two separate mixtures together until you have a favorable consistency. Then form them into a cube shape or round shape.
  4. Get a small portion and form a ball shape using your hands. You can make any shape you prefer like logs, nuggets, cubes, etc.
  5. Repeat the previous step for the rest of the ingredients.
  6. Transfer to a serving plate or tray. Dust with Milo powder using a strainer or a sifter. Serve and enjoy!
Peach Mango Lumpia
  1. Slice the mango and peach into small cubes and put in a container.
  2. Add sugar to the combination of the two and mix.
  3. Put it until it boils and simmer for 15-30 minutes or until the consistency of the syrup it is in is very low.
  4. Let it cool down and prepare your wrapper
  5. Put a portion of the cooked peach mango into the lumpia wrapper and wrap it up.
  6. Fry it in a cooking oil and dry the oil in a separate place.
Potato Bubbles
  1. Wash the potato and slightly cut in the middle. Then boil it in water.
  2. After boiling, put it into an ice bath to peel easier.
  3. Mashed the potato.
  4. Mix the mashed potato with 200 g of all-purpose flour and cornstarch each, black pepper, and salt. Mix until the mixture becomes smooth and moldable.
  5. After molding, cut it into desired size.
  6. Press the tines of a fork into each piece to create a ridged pattern.
  7. Heat the cooking oil.
  8. Fry the potato pieces in 3-5 minutes. Then, drain the excess oil using a strainer.
  9. Fry it again for the second time until golden.
Blue Lemonade
  1. First, we need to create the Blue Curacao Syrup. Grate the orange’s skin while avoiding the white part. Then, get the orange juice.
  2. In a pot, put a ½ cup of sugar and put the grated orange skin and juice. Cook it on a low flame for 8 to 10 minutes or until the syrup is thick. Add a ½ tablespoon of blue food coloring and cook it for another 2 minutes.
  3. Let it cool and strain.
  4. Using a squeezer, squeeze lemon and mint leaves together.
  5. On a cup, add 3 tablespoons of sugar, ice, lemon and mint juice, 2 tablespoons of curacao syrup, and lastly the 16oz of sprite.
Iced Tea
  1. Add 2 cups of water to a pot or pan on the stovetop. Bring water to a boil, turn off heat and immediately add the 2 tea bags to the boiling water. Allow the tea bags to steep for 3 to 5 minutes at most.
  2. Pour the tea into a pitcher and let it cool for 5 to 10 minutes. Once the tea reaches room temperature, add 2 cups of cold water to the pitcher. Stir and refrigerate for 2 hours before serving.
  3. Once the tea is cold, fill glasses with ice cubes for serving.
  4. Lastly, add the lemon and calamansi juice to the tea and garnish with lemon slices.
Strawberry Soda
  1. First, add 2-3 tablespoons of strawberry jam to a 16oz cup
  2. Add ice to the cup
  3. Pour 150 ml of sprite soda until the cup is half full.
  4. And lastly, add 150 ml of Gatorade until the cup is full.
Unicorn Dream Drink
  1. Pour 117.2 mL of blue gatorade and 150 mL mogu mogu lychee in an ice cube tray then freeze for 24 hours.
  2. Take out the flavored iced cube pieces and place it on a plate.
  3. Using the tongs, layer the blue gatorade iced cube pieces on the bottom of the cup followed by the mogu mogu lychee iced cube pieces on top.
  4. Lastly, pour 150 mL of sprite in the cup.
Packaging
  1. The chosen products will be put together in a food packaging with baking paper underneath and divided using food trays to avoid mixing of foods.
  2. For the drinks, each will be placed in a 350 mL cylindrical bottle.
  3. For the sauces, each will be placed in a 10 mL hinged plastic sauce cup.
  4. Lastly, the packaging will be put on a brown paper bag with thank you and instruction card inside and will be sealed using the logo stickers.
The company’s production manager will be the one who's in control of the whole production process to ensure the quality, safety, and cleanliness of the products.

Distribution Logistics

Bundles of Joy will initially conduct a home-based operation and will make use of Facebook, a social networking site, to promote the products and connect to its potential customers. The menu and order form will be posted on Facebook so the customers could see the actual product selection they could choose from and order it online for easier transaction. The company will only accept pre orders and the order form will only be open every Monday to Friday until 12 PM to give ample time for the production team to buy the raw materials and prepare the orders needed to be delivered every Sunday. The customers can choose from the wide variety of modes of delivery offered by the company such as pick up/meet up in Lucky Gold Plaza or Lifehomes in Rosario, Pasig City, bike delivery offered by the company, and the same day delivery couriers such as Lalamove and Grab. The delivery fee except for pick up/meet up will be shouldered by the customers. The customers would also have the freedom to pick their chosen mode of payment such as online payment such as GCash or Paymaya or cash-on-delivery provided that they will submit one (1) valid ID for verification purpose.

Regulatory and Other Compliance Issues

Bundles of Joy will only operate online while the pandemic is still apparent. Having the pandemic in mind, the company will be focusing on ensuring the quality and safety of the products and services that it will offer to its customers to earn their trust. After some time, once the company's business outcome is achieved, the company will acquire the needed documents to legally operate as a small online business such as barangay clearance, mayor’s permit, Bureau of Internal Revenue registration, Department of Trade and Industry registration, and Social Security System registration.

Marketing and Sales Strategy

The market and sales strategy highlights the whole process of ordering and delivery of the food bundles from Bundles of Joy. It is important to have easy access and to have gratifying experience in purchasing the goods and products of the business. We agree on optimizing Facebook as our main social media platform which will be used for promoting our product, ordering of foods and inquiries regarding the products. In addition, we created an accessible and organized website for more convenient purchasing of goods/products among our target market.
Providing consumers quick response is one essential factor in accommodating their orders. Bundles of Joy will use the messenger application and Google forms in acquiring orders to guarantee the customers’ correct requested food combinations and bundle properly. We have a pre-order policy in place, where the team receives orders for a limited number of slots and then delivers them to the customer on a specific day of the week to preserve the quality of the food. Bundles of Joy will assure to accommodate every customer's inquiries as soon as possible to avoid mistakes in purchasing.
Bundles of Joy caters unique food combinations and offers a variety of food sets to satisfy customers' cravings. We offer different bundles consisting of different food combinations and sets and can also be customized depending on the food they desire to choose from the menu list. Each bundle varies its cost depending on what type of food bundle the customer purchased. Bundles of Joy will ensure the excellent quality and affordability of each bundle. The team agreed to manufacture each bundle with eco-friendly and recyclable materials to secure safety among risky additives and not create harm to the environment.
