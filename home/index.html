Mission Statement
Bundles of Joy is a company that aims to cater the trendy food cravings of the general public by offering affordable, high-quality, clean, safe, customizable, and variety of sweet and savory food choices in a food bundle.

Vision Statement
To become the most convenient food company for every Filipino and bring high quality, tasty, and budget-friendly food cravings.

History
Bundles of Joy is a start-up business developed by Group 4 of Grade 12 - Marcov from Pasig City Science High School. The group initially proposed other 5 products and services but found their common ground in a food business.The owners are all digital natives and food-lovers hence the concept of the company which incorporates trendy foods that can be seen in social media in affordable food bundles. They wanted to provide affordable yet high quality trendy foods to satisfy the general public's cravings without burdening their pockets and customising it according to their likings.

Details of the Product & Target Market
Customizable bundles of trendy foods have different varieties of foods that are packed in one container. Most of the food variety are the foods that most of us crave whenever we encounter or it passes through our social media accounts. We cater sweet foods, various common street foods, and unique and new foods. Our products are surely freshly made. Bundles of Joy are perfect for any age from kids to adults because Filipinos are always in the mood for food. Customers can choose from the different varieties of foods we have are listed below:

Menu
Gummy Candies w/ Chamoy Drizzle

different gummy candies like worms and bears mixed with the trending candy, Kiamoy Drizzle.

Milo Pastillas

a Filipino sweet made from milk but added Milo to alter its color and flavor.

Caramel Bars

soft, chewy, and crunchy bar that is made with flour, butter, eggs, vanilla, brown sugar, and nuts.

Graham Cake

an all-time favorite dessert of Filipinos that is made out of graham crackers, sweetened cream, and any kind of fruits that can be topped on it.

Corn Dog

a street food influenced by Koreans. It is a deep-fried hotdogs or sausage skewered on sticks and dipped in a corn batter.

Peach Mango & Choco Mallow Lumpia

is the newest and unique product we offer in Bundle of Joy. Similar to pies offered by different fast food companies but lumpia wrapper is used to cover it.

Unique Selling Point

Bundles of Joy offers tasty and budget friendly trending foods we usually see online. We will also make sure that you will receive high quality products that are guaranteed safe and clean and made efficiently as fast as we can.

Value Proposition

Satisfy your cravings with our unique and customizable food sets according to your taste buds at a very easy and accessible way to order and receive it according to your likings as well.
